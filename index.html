About DotVest

Bridging Traditional Finance and Blockchain

DotVest is revolutionizing African financial markets by leveraging blockchain technology to tokenize stocks, making investment opportunities accessible to a global audience.

Founded in 2024, our mission is to democratize access to African markets, starting with Nigeria's vibrant stock exchange. We're creating a seamless bridge between traditional finance and the blockchain ecosystem.

With our platform, investors worldwide can participate in Africa's economic growth story, while local businesses gain access to unprecedented levels of capital and liquidity.

How It Works

Partnership

We partner with African stock exchanges and regulatory bodies to tokenize listed companies in full compliance with local regulations.

Tokenization

Each stock is converted into blockchain-based tokens that represent fractional or full ownership with complete transparency.

Platform Access

Investors access our secure platform to buy, sell, and trade tokenized stocks with institutional-grade security.

Trade & Grow

Users trade tokens 24/7 with real-time pricing, instant settlement, and automated dividend distribution.

Investor Benefits

Global Access

Invest in African markets from anywhere in the world without traditional brokerage accounts, complex paperwork, or geographical restrictions.

Lower Barriers

Fractional ownership allows investment with smaller amounts, making premium African stocks accessible to retail investors globally.

Instant Settlement

Blockchain technology enables near-instant settlement compared to traditional T+2 or T+3 systems, improving capital efficiency.

Reduced Costs

Eliminate multiple intermediaries and significantly reduce transaction fees, commission costs, and foreign exchange spreads.

24/7 Trading

Trade tokenized stocks around the clock, beyond traditional market hours, providing unprecedented flexibility and liquidity.

Enhanced Security

Blockchain's immutable ledger provides transparent, secure, and tamper-proof ownership records with full audit trails.

Tokenization Process

Our tokenization process transforms traditional stocks into digital assets on the blockchain:

  • Regulatory compliant tokenization framework with full legal backing
  • 1:1 asset-backed tokens representing real shares with voting rights
  • Smart contracts for automated dividend distribution and corporate actions
  • Multi-chain support (Ethereum, Polygon, Binance Smart Chain, Arbitrum)
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Full transparency of token reserves with real-time reporting
  • Institutional custody solutions for large-scale investors
  • Compliance with international securities regulations

Why Invest in African Markets?

Africa represents the world's next major growth frontier with unprecedented opportunities:

  • Fastest growing middle class globally with increasing purchasing power
  • Massive infrastructure development across multiple sectors
  • Young, tech-savvy population driving digital transformation
  • Abundant natural resources and emerging renewable energy sectors
  • Increasing political stability and improved governance frameworks
  • Growing intra-African trade through continental free trade agreements
  • Undervalued markets with significant upside potential

Our platform provides unprecedented access to these opportunities through secure, regulated tokenized assets.
